Astral Intelligence Index (AII)

A single score from 0–100 that measures how cosmically charged the current window is, based on lunar intensity, solar storms, and timing anomalies.

How we calculate charge

  • Lunar intensity — weighting around full + new moons and perigee windows.
  • Phase profile — waxing vs waning dynamics mapped to risk-on / risk-off conditions.
  • Distance factor — closer moon = stronger tide in behaviour & volatility.
  • Timing clusters — historically reactive windows captured as overlays.

Reading the score

  • 0–25 — low charge: chop, accumulation, reset.
  • 25–50 — neutral: flows + news drive more than the sky.
  • 50–75 — charged window: volatility statistically elevated.
  • 75–100 — high-alert: strong probability of outsized moves.
