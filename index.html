How Conspira AI reads the astral tape

The AII is a composite score built from lunar mechanics, geometrics and historical market response during charged windows.

Lunar mechanics

We pull live astronomy data (phase, illumination, rise / set times) from the current sky over Earth. Full and new moon corridors, eclipses and sharp illumination swings are weighted more heavily.

Volatility regimes

Certain phases and windows historically line up with outsized volatility and liquidity imbalances. We treat these as “charged regimes” and amplify them in the index.

Astral stress score

The AII compresses this into a 0–100 score with simple bands: 0–35 calm, 36–69 charged, 70–100 extreme. It’s not directional alpha yet, but it tells you when to respect the tape.
