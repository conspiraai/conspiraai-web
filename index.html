Astral Intelligence Index

A single score from 0–100 that measures how cosmically charged the market window is. It’s meant as an overlay to price and volume data, not a crystal ball.

What goes into the index

  • Lunar intensity – full & new moons, perigee / “supermoon” windows.
  • Eclipse shock – chaos bands around solar & lunar eclipses.
  • Retrograde misfires – fakeouts & failed breakouts during key Mercury retrogrades.
  • Solar storms – geomagnetic Kp spikes and attention-shifting solar events.
  • Portal days – numerology-aligned dates that correlate with liquidity spikes.

How to read the score

  • 0–20 · Low charge – chop, reset, build lists.
  • 20–40 · Neutral – flows & news matter more than stars.
  • 40–70 · Charged – volatility window statistically elevated.
  • 70–100 · High alert – strong probability of outsized moves.
