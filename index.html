Astral Intelligence Index

A single score from 0–100 that measures how cosmically charged the current window is, based on lunar intensity, eclipses, retrogrades, solar storms, and numerology portals.

How it works

  • Lunar intensity — full & new moons, perigee / apogee windows.
  • Eclipse shock — chaos windows around solar & lunar eclipses.
  • Retrograde chaos — misfires & fakeouts during Mercury retrograde.
  • Solar aggro — geomagnetic storms & solar flares.
  • Portal days — numerology-aligned dates & attention spikes.

Reading the index

  • 0–20 — low charge: chop / reset.
  • 20–40 — neutral: flows & news drive price more than stars.
  • 40–70 — charged: volatility window statistically elevated.
  • 70–100 — high alert: strong probability of outsized moves.
